Salesforce OAuth Integration

Salesforce OAuth Integration is a secure application that enables users to authenticate with their Google account and connect Google services to Salesforce using the OAuth 2.0 authorization framework.

Our application is designed to simplify secure access between Salesforce and Google APIs while ensuring users maintain full control over the data they authorize.

What the application does

The application allows users to:

Sign in securely using their Google account.
Grant permission for Salesforce to access approved Google services.
Synchronize authorized Google data with Salesforce.
Revoke access at any time through their Google Account settings.
Why we request Google data

The application requests Google account information only after you explicitly authorize access.

Depending on the permissions granted, the application may access:

Your Google account email address to identify your account.
Basic profile information to personalize your experience.
Additional Google API data only for features that you choose to use.

We do not access or use your Google data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data protection

We are committed to protecting your information by:

Using OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication.
Requesting only the minimum permissions required.
Never selling or sharing your personal information with third parties.
Allowing you to revoke access at any time.
